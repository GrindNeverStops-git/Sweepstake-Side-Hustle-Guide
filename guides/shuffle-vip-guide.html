Shuffle.us VIP Guide

How to climb Shuffle's VIP ranks, grind XP efficiently, and stack free SC through rakeback, bonuses, and bonus codes.

How Shuffle's VIP System Works

Shuffle rewards consistent play through a tiered VIP system. As you move up ranks, you unlock additional reward types on top of each other:

  • Bronze 1 unlocks Instant Rakeback — a small percentage returned automatically as you play
  • Bronze 2 unlocks a Weekly Bonus
  • Silver unlocks a Monthly Bonus

Each rank stacks on top of the last, so by the time you hit Silver you're collecting rakeback, weekly bonuses, and monthly bonuses simultaneously.

Weekly Playthrough Target

Set yourself a goal of playing through 1,000 SC per week. Hitting this target keeps you qualified for the best ongoing bonus codes and keeps your XP climbing steadily toward the next rank.
