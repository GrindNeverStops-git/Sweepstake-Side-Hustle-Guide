Strategy
The full playbook

Six things that separate people who actually cash out from people who don't.

01
Registration & verification
Register with the exact name on your ID — Joseph ≠ Joe. A mismatch will block your redemption when it matters most. Get verified before you deposit or redeem anything. Some sites have issues with online banks, so keep a traditional checking account as your payout method. Set it up early.
02
Washing — playthrough before redemption
Most sites require one playthrough before you can cash out. The goal is to minimize what you lose during that wash.

Best options: High-RTP slots and Plinko (low volatility) for steady returns. Blackjack with basic strategy for slow but reliable results. WowVegas "Happy Hours" give discounted playthrough rates — a significant edge if you time it.
Key principle: $1 × 100 hands beats $20 × 5 hands every time. Volume, not bet size, is how you approach true RTP.
03
Daily farming — efficiency is everything
On PC: Save all daily claim links in a Chrome bookmark folder → right-click the folder → "Open all." Then fly through with Ctrl+Tab and Ctrl+W. 40+ sites in about 5 minutes.

On mobile: Safari tab groups with Face ID logins. Still under 10 minutes. The spreadsheet has every claim link organized by platform.
Geoblock errors? Switch from mobile data to Wi-Fi.
04
Promos — optional but powerful
Sites regularly run sales where you buy coins at a steep discount. Example: a $200 package on sale for $100, or a $40 return on a $25 purchase. These aren't required — you can run this entire strategy for free — but they multiply earnings fast when stacked with your existing dailies.

Rule: Only buy promos you can afford to treat as gone. The edge is real but so is variance.
05
Discipline — the hardest part
Don't blow your dailies chasing a big slot spin. Collect them, combine them with a good promo, wash carefully, and cash out. Boring process = consistent money.

Track your wins and losses in a spreadsheet. It keeps you grounded and makes tax time significantly easier.
06
The bottom line
This isn't about jackpots. It's about stacking dailies, minimizing risk, and cashing out with discipline. With consistency across 50+ sites it's a realistic $750–$1,000/month side stream — sometimes more. Think of it as free gas, groceries, or a year of Netflix for about 5 minutes of effort a day.
