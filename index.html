What is this, exactly?

New here? Read this first. It covers everything you need to know before diving in.

01 What are sweepstakes sites? ▾

They're legal online platforms that let you play casino-style games using virtual coins. They're not real money casinos — they operate under sweepstakes law, which makes them legal in most US states.

02 Why do they give out free coins? ▾

These sites make money when people buy coin packages. To hook new users and keep existing ones logging in daily, they hand out free coins constantly through daily bonuses, login streaks, promos, and more.

03 How does it become real money? ▾

Most sites have two coin types: one just for fun, and one (usually called "Sweeps Coins" or SC) that can be redeemed for real cash or gift cards. The free daily bonuses include the redeemable kind.

04 What's the actual strategy? ▾

Sign up for as many quality sites as possible, claim the free daily bonus on each one, play through the required amount on low-risk games, then cash out. Repeat every day across 50+ sites.

05 Do I have to spend money? ▾

The base strategy is completely free and still pulls consistent money. That said, promo purchases will significantly boost your earnings since you're buying discounted coin packages at a guaranteed edge. Totally optional but highly recommended once you're comfortable with the strategy.

06 Is there any risk? ▾

Virtually no risk following this guide. The daily farming strategy costs nothing and pays out consistently. Promo purchases are extremely safe when used with the strategies here since you're buying discounted value, not gambling blind. Just confirm your state is eligible before signing up.

Strategy
The full playbook

Six things that separate people who actually cash out from people who don't.

01
Registration & verification
Register with the exact name on your ID. Joseph ≠ Joe. A mismatch will block your redemption when it matters most. Get verified before you deposit or redeem anything. Some sites have issues with online banks, so keep a traditional checking account as your payout method. Set it up early.
02
Washing. playthrough before redemption
Most sites require one playthrough before you can cash out. The goal is to get through it while losing as little as possible. Here is exactly how.

Roulette: Bet equal amounts on both red and black simultaneously. You have a 98% chance of getting your money back on every single spin since the only loss is landing on green. This is the easiest and fastest way to burn through playthrough requirements while keeping nearly everything.

Blackjack: Play small hands using basic strategy. Low dollar bets spread across many hands keeps variance near zero. You will slowly grind through the requirement without big swings and can easily break even or come out ahead.

Slots: If you prefer slots, always spin at 10 or 20 cents. Lower bet sizes maximize your RTP and give you far more spins per dollar, meaning more playthrough completed per coin spent.
Key principle: Volume beats bet size every time. 100 small hands beats 5 big ones. Slow and steady clears playthrough with the least damage.
03
Daily farming. efficiency is everything
On PC: Save all daily claim links in a Chrome bookmark folder → right-click the folder → "Open all." Then fly through with Ctrl+Tab and Ctrl+W. 40+ sites in about 5 minutes.

On mobile: Safari tab groups with Face ID logins. Still under 30 minutes. The spreadsheet has every claim link organized by platform.
Geoblock errors? Switch from mobile data to Wi-Fi.
04
Promos. optional but powerful
Sites regularly run sales where you buy coins at a steep discount. Example: a $200 package on sale for $100, or a $40 return on a $25 purchase. These aren't required. you can run this entire strategy for free. but they multiply earnings fast when stacked with your existing dailies.

Rule: Only buy promos you can afford to treat as gone. The edge is real but so is variance.
05
Discipline. the hardest part
Don't blow your dailies chasing a big slot spin. Collect them, combine them with a good promo, wash carefully, and cash out. Boring process = consistent money.

Track your wins and losses in a spreadsheet. It keeps you grounded and makes tax time significantly easier.
06
The bottom line
This isn't about jackpots. It's about stacking dailies, minimizing risk, and cashing out with discipline. With consistency across 50+ sites it's a realistic $750–$1,000/month side stream. sometimes more. Think of it as free gas, groceries, or a year of Netflix for about 5 minutes of effort a day.
